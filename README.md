# LinkedIn Profile Optimizer — Make Your Profile Work While You Sleep

An AI skill that reverse-engineers what top LinkedIn profiles in your field do right, then rewrites yours to match — so recruiters find you, founders get taken seriously, and your profile actually converts visitors into conversations.

**Built for two types of people:**
- **Job seekers** who want their profile to show up in recruiter searches and speak directly to hiring managers
- **Early-stage founders** who need a LinkedIn presence that builds credibility fast — even if they've never posted before

## The Problem

Most LinkedIn profiles are written the way people *think* they should sound — not the way that actually gets results. Job seekers bury their best skills in paragraph-form summaries. Founders write bios that sound like pitch decks instead of people.

Meanwhile, the people who *are* getting recruiter InMails and inbound leads have profiles that follow specific, learnable patterns.

This skill finds those patterns and applies them to your profile.

## What It Does

### For Job Seekers

**1. Competitive Profile Analysis**
Give Sai your resume and 2-3 target job descriptions. Sai researches your target industry and finds:
- Top Voices and thought leaders in your field
- HR/recruiters who hire for roles like yours
- People who recently landed jobs similar to your target
- Job descriptions from your target companies

Then Sai analyzes what their profiles have in common — headlines, summary structure, skill keywords, featured sections, experience descriptions — and maps the gap between their profiles and yours.

**2. Full Profile Rewrite**

Sai delivers section-by-section optimization:

| Section | What Sai Does |
|---------|--------------|
| **Headline** | Rewrites using keyword-rich format that matches recruiter search patterns |
| **About** | Restructures with hook → value proposition → proof points → CTA |
| **Experience** | Converts job descriptions to achievement-driven bullets with metrics |
| **Skills** | Adds missing skills that appear in target JDs (boosts search ranking) |
| **Featured** | Recommends what to pin (projects, articles, portfolio links) |
| **Banner** | Suggests banner text/design that reinforces positioning |

> *Example: Before: "Product Manager at TechCorp" → After: "AI Product Manager | Shipped 3 ML products to 2M+ users | Ex-Google, Ex-Stripe | Building the future of AI-native workflows"*

**3. Recruiter Search Optimization**
LinkedIn's search algorithm weights certain fields heavily. Sai audits your profile against these signals:
- Are your top 3 skills in your headline?
- Does your About section contain the exact phrases recruiters search for?
- Are your experience entries keyword-optimized?
- Is your location set correctly for target job markets?

---

### For Early-Stage Founders

**1. Founder Profile Benchmarking**
Two paths:
- **You know who's doing it well**: Give Sai 3-5 founder profiles you admire. Sai analyzes their headline formulas, content themes, engagement patterns, and profile structure.
- **You don't know where to start**: Tell Sai what your product does. Sai finds 5-10 Top Voices in your space — founders with high follower counts, active posting, and strong engagement — and uses them as benchmarks.

**2. Credibility-First Profile Build**

Early founders often have a credibility gap — you're building something new and nobody knows you yet. Sai structures your profile to close that gap:

| Element | Strategy |
|---------|----------|
| **Headline** | Leads with the problem you solve, not your title |
| **About** | Story arc: personal motivation → what you're building → early traction → what's next |
| **Experience** | Frames previous roles as the origin story for your startup |
| **Featured** | Product demo, press mentions, or a flagship post |
| **Activity** | Recommends 3-5 post topics to publish immediately for social proof |

> *Example: Before: "CEO & Co-Founder at NewCo" → After: "Building [ProductName] — helping SMBs cut invoice processing from 4 hours to 4 minutes | Ex-Stripe Payments | YC W26"*

**3. Top Voice Analysis Report**

Sai delivers a detailed breakdown of each benchmark profile:
- What headline formula they use
- How they structure their About section
- What content topics drive their highest engagement
- Which skills and endorsements they feature
- How often they post and what formats work best

## Quick Start

1. Tell Sai: **"Optimize my LinkedIn profile"**
2. Sai asks:
   - Are you a job seeker or founder?
   - *(Job seeker)* Paste your resume + 2-3 target job links
   - *(Founder)* Describe your product in 2 sentences, or share 3 profiles you admire
3. Sai researches, analyzes, and delivers a complete optimization plan
4. Review the recommendations and tell Sai to apply them — or do it yourself

## Features

- **Competitive analysis** — studies real profiles in your field, not generic templates
- **Section-by-section rewrites** — headline, about, experience, skills, featured
- **Keyword optimization** — matches recruiter search patterns and JD language
- **Benchmark reports** — detailed breakdown of top profiles in your industry
- **Before/after comparison** — see exactly what changed and why
- **Founder mode** — credibility-building framework for early-stage founders
- **Top Voice discovery** — finds the most active, high-engagement profiles in any niche
- **Action items** — specific, ranked list of changes from highest to lowest impact
- **Tone preservation** — rewrites sound like you, not like a LinkedIn template
- **Privacy-first** — all analysis happens locally, your resume stays on your machine

## How It Works

1. **Input**: You provide your resume/background + target roles or product description
2. **Research**: Sai browses LinkedIn to study benchmark profiles, job descriptions, and industry patterns
3. **Analysis**: Sai maps the gap between your current profile and the top performers
4. **Optimization**: Sai generates section-by-section rewrites with explanations
5. **Review**: You approve changes, Sai applies them (or you copy-paste)

## Privacy

- Your resume and profile data stay on your local machine
- Sai browses LinkedIn using your own browser session — no credentials are stored
- No data is sent to third-party services
- Research results are cached locally for your reference

## Requirements

- [Sai by Simular](https://simular.ai/sai) installed on your computer
- An active LinkedIn account with browser session
- Your resume or CV (for job seekers)
- Your product description (for founders)
